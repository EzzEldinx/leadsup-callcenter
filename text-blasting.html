Common Questions.

Straight answers to what everyone asks before they start.

Is text blasting legal? +

Yes — when it's done right. Our entire setup process is built around A2P 10DLC compliance and TCPA safety. We register your brand with the carriers, get your campaign approved, handle opt-outs properly, and only message contacts from legally-sourced lists. The reason most people say it's "risky" is because they're using unregistered numbers and getting flagged. That's exactly the problem we solve.

Do I need my own phone numbers or software? +

No. We provide everything — the numbers, the platform, the compliance infrastructure, and the team replying to messages. You don't need to buy, configure, or maintain anything. All you do is provide your business details for registration and approve our messaging scripts.

How fast can I launch? +

Launch timelines depend on A2P 10DLC registration, which is a carrier-enforced process we can't shortcut — and any provider who claims they can is cutting corners that'll get your numbers flagged. We move as fast as the carriers allow, and once your campaign is approved, messages go out within days. That's why we recommend starting the onboarding process before your current marketing cools off.

What happens if someone replies angry or opts out? +

Opt-outs are handled automatically and instantly — it's built into the compliance layer. Angry replies? Our team handles them professionally, logs them, and removes the contact. Your reputation stays clean. You never see a single unhappy conversation unless you specifically ask to review them.

How do I actually get the leads? +

Qualified leads are delivered directly to your CRM (free with every package) with the full conversation context. You see what they said, what they're interested in, and when they're ready to talk. You pick up the phone or email — we've done the hard part of finding someone who actually wants to hear from you.

Can I combine this with your cold calling service? +

Absolutely — and honestly, that's when this works best. Text blasting fills the top of your funnel with warm prospects; cold calling closes them. Clients who run both services together consistently see higher close rates because they're hitting prospects on multiple channels with context. Ask about our bundled packages.
