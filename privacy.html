DATA & SECURITY

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 8, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to LeadsUp Call Center. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you interact with us through our website, calls, SMS, and email communications. Our goal is to ensure your information is handled responsibly and transparently as we provide our outbound marketing and lead generation services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal details as part of our communication and lead generation services:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and mailing address.
  • Business Information: Company name, job title, and industry details.
  • Interaction Data: Call logs, call duration, responses, and notes from communications with our agents.
2.2 Technical Data

When you visit our website or digital platforms, we may automatically collect:

  • Device & Usage Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and engagement metrics.
  • Tracking Data: Information collected through cookies or analytics tools to enhance your website experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Our Services: Conduct outbound marketing, cold calling, and appointment-setting campaigns.
  • Deliver Market Insights: Send personalized market research and reports requested through our website.
  • Communicate With You: Respond to inquiries, provide updates, or share offers related to LeadsUp services.
  • Improve Performance: Evaluate and optimize our outreach strategies, call quality, and data accuracy.
  • Ensure Compliance: Fulfill legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations.

4. Consent to Receive SMS & Emails

By submitting your information through our website or forms, you consent to receive communications (including emails, SMS, and calls) from LeadsUp Call Center regarding services, offers, updates, or follow-ups.

You can withdraw your consent at any time by:

  • Replying "STOP" to any SMS message.
  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • Contacting us directly at support@leadsupcallcenter.com.

We respect your communication preferences and ensure your consent choices are updated promptly.

5. How We Share Your Information

We may share your data in the following situations:

  • With Clients: When providing lead generation or marketing services on their behalf.
  • With Trusted Vendors: For data verification, analytics, or technical support — under confidentiality agreements.
  • For Legal or Compliance Reasons: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our company, clients, or others.

6. Data Security

We implement advanced security measures — encryption, restricted access, and monitoring — to protect your data from unauthorized use or disclosure. While no system is 100% secure, we take all reasonable steps to maintain the safety of your information.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access & Correct: Request access to or updates of your personal information.
  • Opt-Out: Stop receiving SMS, emails, or calls at any time.
  • Request Deletion: Ask for your data to be deleted, subject to legal and contractual requirements.

For requests or questions, contact support@leadsupcallcenter.com.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect new practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Your continued use of our services implies acceptance of the revised terms.
